Run Dino, Run!

Immerse yourself in Dino's thrilling Jurassic adventure! In the reincarnation of the well-known game, you have to jump over cacti, stalakites, spikes, and other obstacles in 7 different locations! Feel like a dino and get an unforgettable experience! Run or die, the choice is not great. How long would you spend in the Jurassic period?

Become the best of the best

Compare your skills with other players around the world. Can you top the leaderboard? Explore the world of Dino like no one else dreamed of, become the best dino in the Jurassic, and take your well-deserved place on the leaderboard if you can, because only the best can :)

How to install? (Android)

  1. Download .apk file from this site or itch.io
  2. Allow installation from unknown sources (Settings-> Security)
  3. Open the downloaded file
  4. Click "Install"
  5. Google services will tell you that the publisher
    of the application is not known, click "continue anyway"
  6. Enjoy the game
